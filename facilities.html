PRE PRESS

We use Computer-to-Plate (CTP) thermal imaging system, which produces first generation dots with sharper images. We work on softwares like Adobe In- Design, Illustrator, Photoshop, PageMaker, Corel-Draw. For Imposition we use Kodak-preps.

The Computer-to-Plate (CTP) process circumvents the need to photolithographs, thus enormously improving the production process, as printing error corrections can be made directly on the computer. Our familiarity with the process, equipment, and type of plates, file format and preparation all play a role in the success of the computer-to-plate process. We understand that you need the best technology to produce the best output and hence we are best equipped to offer you printing solutions that are fast and long lasting.
